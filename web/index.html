The History Of Music Boxes

The music box was probably invented about 1770 in Switzerland. The earliest music boxes were small enough to be enclosed in a pocket watch, but they were gradually built in larger sizes and housed in rectangular wooden boxes. Until the 1900, music boxes grew in popularity by leaps and bounds.By 1910, however, music boxes had been largely replaced by the phonograph. Now they remain novelty items but we are here to ensure that they make a return.

Features

  • Lossless audio

    Using our proprietary Bluetooth standard, we deliver the highest quality sound that you have ever heard

  • Industrial design

    Inspired by nature and classic curves of vintage screwdrivers, we came up with this unusual and boxy design update for the classis music box

  • Dancing robot

    Using AI analysis of the song, we determine what dance the robot should do for the song and robot performs it with ease.This robot is a marvel to behold

  • Built in Premium DAC

    Plug in your high impedance headphones into NeUbox and discover a whole new dimension of music with AI upscaling of music.

    We decided to adopt the USB-C charging standard for our device charging so carrying a specialized cable is one less thing that you need to worry about.
